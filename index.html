LogicalBite:

LogicalBite is an informative, user-interactive site that takes gives information about the environmental impacts of certain foods. With a few simple clicks, the user may choose as many unprocessed food items that make up their meal and watch with amazement as the site automatically calculates the meal's overall carbon footprint.

Usability Note:

Start out by clicking on one of the major food groups above. On the next page, simply click a product from the list to add it to your ingredients list. Use the back button to go back to the previous page, or the submit button to finalize your selections.
